Food calculator

As you know you should eat a maximum of 1,8kg of food a day on the ship. With the calculator below you can calculate if you have to ration your food more so you can make it to Mars without starving.

All you have to do is type in how much food is left (in kg) and how much longer it takes to get to Mars (in Days).
